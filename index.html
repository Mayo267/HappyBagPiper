The Happy Bagpiper: Bagpipe Performance, Instruction, Retail, and Celtic Culture (LLC) is a company devoted to teaching and performance on the Scottish Great Highland Bagpipe, also known as the Irish Warpipe. Based in the heart of the Philadelphia metropolitan area, The Happy Bagpiper offers:

The very highest-quality bagpipe performances for weddings, parties, funerals / memorial services, corporate celebrations and promotional events, and a wide range of other occasions.

Premium instruction from the area’s only full-time professional bagpipe teacher, with proven methods for helping child, adolescent, and adult learners — even those with little or no musical experience — achieve their bagpipe playing and performance goals.

The Happy Bagpiper also sells retail bagpipes and bagpiping supplies for learning and performance, including practice chanters, tutor books and interactive materials, bagpipes, reeds, and a range of other accessories. The Happy Bagpiper is proud of its in-person service to the greater Philadelphia metro area, including Delaware, Maryland, Eastern Pennsylvania, and New Jersey, but has happy clients as far away as Germany and Japan via the Internet and mail!

The Man Behind The Music

Thom is a highly trained musician-bagpiper with over 3 decades of experience as a highly successful solo and pipe band competitor on the Great Highland Bagpipe, with hundreds of trophies and medals in his record of competitions sanctioned by the Eastern United States Pipe Band Association (EUSPBA) and the Pipers and Pipe Band Society of Ontario, Canada (PPBSO).
A certified, successful grade 1 solo competitor (grade 5 being the lowest) and former member of more than a dozen competition-level pipe bands, Thom is a sought-after performer and bagpiping instructor for individuals and pipe bands. Thom has been the official Instructor to the Camden County (NJ) Emerald Society Pipes & Drums for over 8 years and has provided workshops and seminars to bands and individual bagpipers during that time.
Moving away from a successful executive medical publishing career, Thom decided in 2016 to devote himself full-time to playing and teaching the bagpipe. He established a new business, The Happy Bagpiper LLC, which offers bagpiping performance and instruction services as well as a full range of bagpipes and accessories for sale.
